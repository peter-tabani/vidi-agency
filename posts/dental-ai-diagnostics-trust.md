# The "Used Car Salesman" Fear

It's the most frustrating moment in dentistry: You diagnose a clear need for a crown. You show the patient the X-ray. They nod, say "I'll think about it," and leave—never to return.

Why? **The Trust Gap.**

In 2025, patients are more skeptical than ever. When you recommend treatment, 40% of them secretly wonder if you are just trying to "upsell" them to pay for your new boat.
